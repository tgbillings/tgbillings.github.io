About Me
About Me

Hi, I’m Teddy Billings.

I am a UX designer and Researcher, with front end development skills. My favorite part of the design process is interpreting and visualizing user research and feedback into my designs. Human behavior is constantly changing which means Research needs to be used in 2 main ways: (1) Adaptively: to iterate on a product and to find a beautiful solution to fit users’ needs and (2) Predictively: to innovate and create experiences that pushes the boundaries of both users expectations and technology, giving the design the competative advantage. It is finding this balance of pushing innovation and adapting that I love, and that creates fool proof products.

I am currently living in NYC and working for Pearson, designing products for K -12 Students and teachers. I graduated Cornell University in the Spring of 2017 with a Bachelors of Science in Human Centered Interactions within the department of Information Science, and a double minor in Communications and Technology and Computer Science.
