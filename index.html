About Me

Hi, I’m Teddy Billings.

I am a UX designer and researcher. My favorite part of the design process is interpreting and visualizing user research and feedback into my designs. Human behavior is constantly changing which means research needs to be used in 2 main ways: (1) Adaptively: to iterate on a product and to find a beautiful solution to fit users’ needs and (2) Predictively: to innovate and create experiences that pushes the boundaries of both users' expectations and technology, giving the design a competitive advantage. It is finding this balance of pushing innovation and adapting that I love, and that creates fool-proof products.

I am currently living in Boston and working for Curriculum Associates, designing and researching products for teachers and students. I love cats, videogames and working out (in that order).
